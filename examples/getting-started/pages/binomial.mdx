## Binomial Theorem

In this page, We will introduce binomial theorem.

<M.Theorem name="Binomial Theorem">

  <M.TRef label="binomial" />

  Suppose $n \in \mathbb{N}$, then

  $$
    (a + b)^n = \sum_{k = 0}^n {}_n C_r a^k b^{n - k}
  $$
</M.Theorem>

<M.TRef use="binomial" /> is useful.
